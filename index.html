About Us

Powerss was born from the pain we had in finding great, usable imagery. And we weren’t alone. Which is why, today—millions of creators from around the world have downloaded over 2 billion images to create presentations, artwork, mockups, and more. Powerss is a platform powered by an amazing community that has gifted hundreds of thousands of their own photos to fuel creativity around the world. We’re the place where creators meet their audience, where individuals become a community and where anyone can become a source for creativity. We're a nonprofit organization and rely on supporters like you to help us.
